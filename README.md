### ***Description***
#### Boxinggram is a social media app for boxers, Share photos and videos related to boxing.. like and comment on other boxers or coaches posts.
***

### ***Technologies Used***
* Node.js
* React.js
* Express
* PostgresSQL
* Sequelize
* React Bootstrap
***
